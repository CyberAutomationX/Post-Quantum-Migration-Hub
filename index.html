Recommended starting point: download the workbook, complete the Crypto Inventory sheet for priority systems, then use the playbook and checklists to build a risk-based migration roadmap.

Five-step migration preparation workflow

StepOutcomePractical output
1. InventoryKnown cryptographic dependencies are documented across applications, services, cloud/IAM, PKI, network, code signing, and OT/ICS.Completed Crypto Inventory Template with evidence links.
2. PrioritizeSystems are scored by quantum vulnerability, data shelf life, exposure, criticality, safety impact, and crypto agility.Prioritized P0/P1/P2 backlog and migration blockers register.
3. DesignTarget architecture separates business logic from cryptographic algorithms and relies on supported standards, libraries, protocols, and suppliers.Target-state architecture, supplier roadmap, and test plan.
4. PilotRepresentative non-production systems validate interoperability, certificate size, performance, logging, and rollback.Pilot report with acceptance criteria and decision record.
5. OperationalizeControls, procurement language, evidence retention, monitoring, and exception management are updated for continuing crypto agility.Updated policy, runbooks, dashboards, and recurring inventory process.

NIST-aligned scope

AreaHub alignment
NIST PQC standardsUses FIPS 203 for ML-KEM key establishment planning, FIPS 204 for ML-DSA signature planning, and FIPS 205 for SLH-DSA signature planning.
Crypto agilityPromotes configurable algorithms, approved libraries, dependency abstraction, certificate lifecycle automation, interoperability testing, and operational continuity.
Cybersecurity risk governanceOrganizes readiness around governance, identification, protection, detection, response, and recovery outcomes.
Supply chainIncludes supplier roadmap questions, support-window tracking, product cryptography disclosure, and update-path validation.
Operational technologyApplies a safety-first change-control approach for OT/ICS/SCADA environments, including lab validation and maintenance-window governance.
